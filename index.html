Server Rulebook

Read carefully to ensure a fun and fair experience for everyone.

1. Respect all players and staff. No harassment, racism, or bigotry.
2. Do not exploit bugs or glitches. Report them immediately.
3. Keep conversations in-character (IC) unless in designated OOC chats.

1. No RDM (Random Death Match) or VDM (Vehicle Death Match).
2. You must value your life at all times (NVL).
3. Metagaming (using OOC info IC) is strictly forbidden.

If your character is killed, you cannot remember the events leading to your death or seek revenge. You must avoid the area of your death for at least 15 minutes.
